Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service

What is TempMailPro?

TempMailPro is a free temporary email service that provides disposable email addresses. These addresses can be used to receive emails without exposing your real email address, protecting your privacy and reducing spam.

Is it really free?

Yes, TempMailPro is completely free to use. There are no hidden charges or premium features. Our service is supported by advertisements.

Can I send emails from TempMailPro?

No, TempMailPro is a receive-only service. You can only receive emails at your temporary address, not send them.

Is it anonymous?

Yes, we don't require any personal information to use our service. We don't track or store your IP address or any identifying information.

How long are emails stored?

Emails are stored for 24 hours after they arrive in your inbox. Your temporary email address expires after 10 minutes of inactivity, but you can extend this duration.
